Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

OpenOTP does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All OTP tokens and user data are stored locally on your device. The app does not transmit data to any server and does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

This app operates fully offline and does not require a user account or network access.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the developer via info@openotp.lol.
